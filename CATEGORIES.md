# 🏷️ Repository Category Definitions

---

- **Owned**: Repositories created or owned directly by MetaphyKing.
- **Forked**: Forks of external repositories managed within the profile.
- **Starred**: Public repositories marked as interesting or valuable.
- **Contributed**: Repositories where significant contributions (commits, PRs) have been made.

> **Order of Precedence:** Owned > Forked > Starred > Contributed.
